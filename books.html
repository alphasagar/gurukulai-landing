Practice & Strategy

Practice Materials

• Previous Year Question Papers (PYQs) — minimum last 10 years
• Test Series — pick one institute and stick to it
• Daily MCQs — consistency over volume

Preparation Strategy

  1. Start with NCERTs → move to standard books
  2. Make short notes (one page per topic)
  3. Revise multiple times (3–4 revisions minimum)
  4. Solve PYQs — they reveal UPSC's exact pattern
  5. Take mock tests under real exam conditions

Essay Paper

Essay (GS Paper — General Studies)

No specific book — practice is the only key.
Sources

Other Important Sources

Must-Follow Resources

Mains Preparation Strategy

  1. Previous Year Mains papers (last 10 years) — mandatory
  2. Answer writing practice — daily or every alternate day
  3. Join one focused test series (Vision IAS, ForumIAS, Insights)
  4. Revise notes at least 3–4 times before Mains
  5. Keep examples and data handy for every GS topic
